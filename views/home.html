Nonprofit funding is getting cut. There’s never been a more important time for community members to step up. Your community need you to make it through.

Our mission at SponsorLane is to enable community organizers to build their communities by connecting them to resources.

We’ve already mapped out 200 Bay Area companies that generously sponsor community based events. And, 200 nonprofits have already started using us to find things like food, beverages, and gift cards.
